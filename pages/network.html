What We Install

Complete network solutions for every business type

Every installation backed by Cisco CCNA certification. No guesswork — professional-grade results every time.

📷

Security Camera Systems

Professional HD and 4K IP camera installation for homes, restaurants, retail and offices. Camera layout designed for maximum coverage, clean cable runs, remote phone viewing, motion alerts, night vision and 30+ day recording. Protect your property and staff 24/7.

📡

WiFi 6 Installation & Optimization

Dead zones and dropped connections end here. Wireless site survey of your building, optimal access point placement for full coverage, enterprise-grade WiFi 6 equipment, separate networks for staff, guests and IoT devices. Hotels, restaurants, offices and warehouses — we cover them all.

🔧

Cisco Router & Switch Setup

Professional CCNA-standard router and switch configuration — proper IP addressing, DHCP, DNS, VLANs for traffic segmentation, QoS for voice and video priority, and redundant connections. Your network configured right the first time, not patched together by guesswork.

🔥

Firewall & Cybersecurity

Enterprise-grade firewall with intrusion detection, content filtering, VPN for secure remote access, and automatic firmware updates. Cyber attacks on small businesses are increasing every year — an unprotected network is a liability. Sleep knowing your data is protected.

🍽️

Restaurant Networks & POS

POS terminals, kitchen displays, card machines, online ordering tablets, customer WiFi and cameras all working simultaneously. Separate VLANs for each system so one problem never affects the others. Designed for the busiest moments of your restaurant rush.

🏥

Healthcare & Professional Offices

Medical clinics, dental offices, law firms and accountants need extra security. HIPAA-ready and PIPEDA-compliant network environments with encrypted traffic, isolated workstation networks, guest WiFi separated from internal systems, and comprehensive access logging.
