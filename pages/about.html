Meet the Founder

The person behind UV Technologies AI

Uchit Patel founded UV Technologies AI in Moosomin, Saskatchewan in 2025 with a clear mission — to bring enterprise-level technology to Prairie Canada businesses at honest, local prices.

Growing up and working in Saskatchewan, Uchit saw the same story everywhere — great local businesses held back by outdated websites, unreliable networks and data that nobody knew how to use. He built UV Technologies AI to fix all three problems at once.

With a Cisco CCNA certification and deep expertise in AI systems, web development and data analytics, Uchit personally oversees every project. The 29-agent AI system he built works around the clock — doing in hours what a traditional agency team of 10 would do in weeks. The savings go directly to clients.

When you work with UV Technologies AI, you work with Uchit directly. There are no junior account managers or offshore teams — just a highly capable local professional who is personally invested in your success.

Our Story

Built for Prairie businesses by a Prairie business

Look around Moosomin, Weyburn, Estevan, Virden. Excellent businesses, hardworking owners, loyal communities — but websites from 2012, WiFi that drops during the busiest hour, and years of business data sitting in spreadsheets nobody has ever analysed.

Meanwhile a restaurant in Toronto has a cutting-edge website, enterprise-grade network and a business intelligence dashboard. A dental clinic in Vancouver has a website that ranks #1 on Google and books patients automatically.

That gap makes no sense. Technology does not care about geography — and Prairie Canada businesses deserve the same tools. UV Technologies AI was built to deliver exactly that.

Our 29-agent AI system does in hours what a traditional agency team of 10 does in weeks. We pass those savings on to our clients — professional results at Prairie-friendly prices.
